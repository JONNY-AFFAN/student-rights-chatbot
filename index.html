📜 Punjab Students ke Haq (2026 Latest)

Punjab Free & Compulsory Education Act 2014 – Section 16(4)

"The teacher or incharge of a school shall ensure that a child studying in the school is not subjected to corporal punishment or harassment."

Hitting, slapping, stick, ear pulling, mental torture – sab banned!

2025 School Education Department Orders

  • Sticks completely banned in all schools (even staff room mein nahi rakh sakte)
  • Illegal fines (extra fees) not allowed
  • Teacher par FIR, suspension, dismissal ho sakta hai
  • Child Protection & Welfare Bureau (CPWB) 1121 helpline
